Career Guidance — Nairobi Job Market
Expert CV Writing Tips for the Kenyan Job Market

A compelling CV is your first opportunity to impress a Nairobi employer. Our recruitment team shares proven, Kenya-specific CV writing tips that increase shortlisting rates — from formatting and keyword optimisation to tailoring your profile for each role.

  • Lead with a clear, keyword-rich professional summary
  • List achievements with measurable outcomes, not just duties
  • Keep your CV to two pages — concise and scannable
  • Tailor each application to the specific job description
  • Include verified references available on request
